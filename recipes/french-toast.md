- 2 eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- pinch of salt
- 1/2 tbsp sugar
- pinch of nutmeg
- 1/4 cup whipping cream
- 1/3 cup milk

Mix ingredients together, cut brioche and dip each side in the mixture for three seconds. Fry in butter until golden brown
